provide reports for the roles of the accounts

customer (role='user')

1. Report on list of job requests and completed jobs for a time period for this account.
2. Report on payment status for the jobs in a time period for this account.

role='dispatcher'

1. Report on completed jobs for a time period for all the customer accounts.
2. Report on payment status for the jobs in a time period for all the accounts.
3. Report performance on a trucking or all trucking companies (total number, delivery, how many times they rejected quotes, acceptance rate)

Trucking providers (role='carrier')

Report on fulfilled jobs for a time period.
Report on payment status for the fulfilled jobs in a time period.
